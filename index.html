About the Doctor

Dr. Carl Gemayel

Dr. Carl Gemayel is a licensed physiotherapist with over 12 years of clinical experience, specializing in musculoskeletal rehabilitation, sports injury recovery, and post-surgical care. He obtained his Master's degree in Physical Therapy from the Lebanese University and furthered his training with advanced certifications in Manual Therapy and Sports Medicine.

His philosophy centers on a holistic, patient-first approach — combining precise clinical assessment with hands-on treatment to deliver lasting results. Dr. Gemayel has worked with professional athletes, post-operative patients, and individuals managing chronic pain, tailoring every treatment plan to the individual's unique needs and goals.

Whether you are recovering from an injury, managing a long-term condition, or looking to optimize your physical performance, Dr. Gemayel's clinic provides expert, compassionate care in a modern clinical environment.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Physiotherapy Services

Every treatment plan is individually designed, evidence-based, and focused on achieving the best possible outcome for each patient.

🏃

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Expert recovery programs for athletes of all levels — from acute sports injuries to performance optimization, designed to get you back in the game faster and stronger.

🦴

Back & Neck Pain Treatment

Targeted assessment and treatment of spinal conditions using a combination of manual therapy, therapeutic exercise, and postural correction techniques.

🔬

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Structured rehabilitation programs following orthopaedic procedures, including joint replacements and ligament reconstructions, to restore full function safely.

🤲

Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilisation, soft tissue manipulation, and myofascial release to reduce pain and restore normal movement patterns.

💪

Strength & Mobility Training

Customised exercise programmes that improve functional strength, flexibility, and movement quality — ideal for injury prevention and long-term wellbeing.

📋

Initial Assessment & Screening

A comprehensive biomechanical and functional assessment to identify the root cause of your symptoms and create a clear, personalised treatment roadmap.
